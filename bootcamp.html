Curriculum (5 sessions)

Day 1 — From Topic to Question

  • Brainstorm → narrow → feasibility check (literature & data)
  • Hypothesis vs aim; operationalizing variables
  • Mini-task: convert 3 broad ideas into testable questions

Day 2 — Data & Method

  • Primary vs secondary data; sampling basics
  • Design a short survey/interview or pick official datasets
  • Mini-task: data plan + ethics checklist

Day 3 — Descriptive Stats

  • Mean, median, mode; spreads; simple charts
  • Hands-on in Sheets: compute and visualize
  • Mini-task: 6-chart insight board

Day 4 — Relationships

  • Correlation (and why it isn’t causation)
  • Trend lines; caution with small samples
  • Mini-task: interpret relationships and limits

Day 5 — Write-up & Citations

  • Structure for EE/IA; answering the question with evidence
  • Referencing styles; build bibliography
  • Mini-task: 250-word draft conclusion + reference list
