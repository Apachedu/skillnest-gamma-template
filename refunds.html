1) Eligibility windows

  • Early cancellation (≥ 7 days before cohort start): 100% refund (minus payment-gateway fees) or 100% course credit valid for 6 months.
  • Standard cancellation (3–6 days before start): 50% refund (minus gateway fees) or 100% credit valid for 6 months.
  • Late cancellation (≤ 48 hours before start): No refund. You may defer to a later cohort once within 6 months (₹500 admin fee) or transfer the seat to another student at no cost.

2) After the cohort starts

  • 0–1 session attended and you email us within 24 hours of the first session: 50% credit valid for 6 months.
  • 2 or more sessions attended: no refund or credit.

3) No-show

Missing all sessions without prior notice results in no refund. We will share available materials/recordings where applicable.

4) If we cancel or reschedule

If we cancel a cohort or cannot provide a reasonable alternative, you may choose a 100% refund or a free reschedule.

5) How refunds are paid

  • Refunds are returned to the original payment method (Razorpay/UPI/card).
  • Processing time: 5–7 business days after approval.
  • Gateway fees, FX differences, and taxes are non-refundable as providers do not return them.
  • Credits are non-transferable and expire after 6 months.

6) Exceptional cases

Medical emergencies or exam schedule changes: email brief proof within 7 days — we may issue a full credit at our discretion.

7) How to request

Email contact@skillnestedu.com with the student name, cohort, payment reference, and reason. We reply within 24 hours.
