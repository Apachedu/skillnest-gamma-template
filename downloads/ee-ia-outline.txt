IB EE (suggested structure)
1. Title page
2. Abstract (if your school asks)
3. Contents
4. Introduction (context + focused Research Question)
5. Method (data sources, tools, assumptions, limitations)
6. Analysis (tables, graphs, calculations)
7. Discussion/Evaluation (what the evidence means, weaknesses, alternatives)
8. Conclusion (answer the RQ directly)
9. References (consistent style)
10. Appendices (survey, raw tables, formulas)
